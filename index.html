Background

Work

Software engineering peaked my interest from an early age. I started off by building simple hobby apps and games to now having worked in the mobile space for the last 10+ years. I thrive in fast-paced work environments and love to see how my work touches the lives of people that use the software I build.

Life

Exploring Boston and looking for new places to eat and drink is my favorite past time. I also love to cook and will often spend my weekends perfecting a recipe or trying something wildly new.
